# Managing Leads in Salesforce CRM

## Overview
Leads represent potential customers who have shown interest in products or services.

## Creating a Lead
1. Navigate to the Leads section.
2. Click New.
3. Enter lead details such as name, company, and contact information.
4. Save the lead.

## Updating Lead Status
- Open the lead record.
- Update the status as the lead progresses through the sales process.
