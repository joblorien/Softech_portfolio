## Overview
Salesforce CRM is a cloud-based Software as a Service (SaaS) platform designed to help organizations manage customer relationships, sales processes, and business workflows from a single system.

The platform enables teams to track leads, manage opportunities, automate processes, and gain insights through real-time reporting and dashboards.

## Target Users
Salesforce CRM is used by:
- Sales Representatives
- Sales Managers
- Customer Support Teams
- Marketing Teams
- System Administrators

## Key Capabilities
- Lead and opportunity management
- Customer data tracking and management
- Workflow automation
- Role-based access and permissions
- Reports and dashboards for business insights

## Product Scope
This documentation provides a high-level overview of Salesforce CRM functionality and is intended for learning and portfolio demonstration purposes.

It does not represent official Salesforce documentation.

## Getting Started
1. Users log in to Salesforce CRM using organization credentials.
2. Customer and lead information is added to the system.
3. Sales activities and opportunities are tracked.
4. Reports and dashboards are used to monitor performance.
